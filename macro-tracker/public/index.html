Set your goals
These settings calculate your personalized calorie and macro targets. Here's what each field means:
♂♀
Sex, Age, Height & Weight
Used to calculate your BMR (basal metabolic rate) — the calories your body burns at rest — using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, the gold standard used by dietitians.
⚡
Activity level
Multiplies your BMR to get your TDEE — total daily energy expenditure. Be honest here; most people overestimate their activity level.
🎯
Goal & weekly rate
Losing 0.5–1 lb/week is sustainable and recommended by most dietitians. 1.5 lbs/week is aggressive — only for short-term cuts. Gaining is set conservatively to minimize fat gain.
🥩
Macro split
How your calories are divided between protein, carbs, and fat. High protein (40/30/30) is ideal for body recomposition. Your targets update automatically as you change any field.
